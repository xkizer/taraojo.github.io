Aston University LSS - Loan System

June 2013 - September 2013

I completed an internship at AASE, I worked on a project with the Aston University Languages and Social Sciences (LSS) department to digitize their paper-based loan system.

I worked with the LSS department to design a system that worked on the desktop within their book and DVD area, this system enables users to check in/out books and DVDs.

Greater Birmingham & Solihull LEP - Strategy for Growth

February 2013 - September 2013

While working for AASE I also worked on a project with the Greater Birmingham and Solihull Local Enterprise Partnership (GBS LEP). The aim of this project was to create a digital version of their strategy for growth.

The GBS LEP wanted to reach their audience with a different approach, I worked with them to design an online experience that also enabled them to update their content via a content management system.
